ABOUT DRESSMAKER WORKS

Our Dressmakers will discuss with client requirements and using fabric samples and pattern books offer advice on which may produce the best results. Once the client has decided on a style, our dressmaker will proceed with their works in taking measurements, and either adapts an existing pattern or produces a pattern unique to that client. She / He will work out the cost of the work, based on the fabric and trimmings needed and the amount of time the work is likely to take. Dressmakers also offer an alteration and repair service.

NEED SOME SPACE FOR YOUR SEWING PROJECT

Just need some space to cut, sew, or work with a dress form? We now offer a drafting and sewing workroom rental option in our classroom so you can reserve a cutting table, mannequin, or a machine to use by the hour. This a fun and affordable way to practice independently in the shop and further your sewing skills, or just stretch out and take advantage of the tall tables and equipment we have here.

Rentals are calculated in one hour increments, so if you use a space for 1.5 hours, the fee will be for 2 hours

You can also add a workroom rental after your event ended to complete your project by approach our Administrator or Instructor half hour in advance as long as it is not currently in use by another person or no next class available

Housekeeping is required during your work or after use the workroom.
